Who we are

A mother and son with a genuine love for second-hand clothing.

Lost & Found started the way most good ideas do — out of something we were already doing anyway. For over a decade, Noah and Nita have been hitting op shops, markets, and thrift stores together across Auckland, building an eye for quality pieces and a deep appreciation for clothes that have a story.

Noah brings hands-on experience from years working in surf and skate fashion retail — understanding what moves, what lasts, and what's worth holding onto. That background shapes how we assess every collection and what ends up finding a new home.

We started Lost & Found because we genuinely believe great clothing shouldn't end up in a bin. Come as you are, bag it up, and leave it to us.
