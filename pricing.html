Condition guide
Condition is the biggest factor in your offer. Here's what each grade means.
ConditionGradeWhat it looks like
Near new×1.0Tags still on, or clearly never worn. No signs of washing.
Excellent×0.85Worn 1–3 times max. No fading, no pilling, no marks.
Good×0.65Normal wear. Colour intact. No visible damage or stains.
Fair×0.40Minor fading around collar, light pilling. Still presentable.
Poor×0.10Visible damage, stains, heavy pilling, or structural issues. Likely donation only.
