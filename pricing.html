The three grades

Every item gets sorted on the spot.

We make one overall grade decision for your collection — you'll know exactly what you're getting before we take anything.

Premium grade
Valuable & resellable clothing
$5–15
per piece · assessed individually
Reserved for pieces with real resale value — brands people actually seek out, condition that holds up, styles that move. These are assessed one by one, not lumped together. Men's pieces are especially sought after right now.

Standard grade
General adult clothing
$3–7
per kg · whole collection, one price
Most collections fall here. Wearable adult clothing in mixed condition — no standout pieces, but clean and sellable. We weigh the lot and pay a flat rate per kilogram. Simple, fast, no haggling.
What qualifies
Everyday adult men's and women's clothing Good condition, no major damage Mixed labels and styles Tops, bottoms, dresses, knitwear Coats and jackets without brand premium
A typical wardrobe clear-out — 3 bags of mixed adult clothing — usually lands here.

Donated free

What we don't collect

Clothing only — and we're strict about it.

We focus entirely on adult clothing because that's where we can do our job properly. Everything else is outside our scope.

👟
No shoes or footwear
We don't collect shoes, boots, sandals, or any footwear — even high-end brands. This isn't something we'll make exceptions on.
👜
No bags or accessories
Handbags, backpacks, belts, hats, scarves — not accepted. Clothing only means clothing only.
🏠
No homewares or linen
Bedding, towels, curtains, cushions — none of it. We're a clothing service, not a general op shop collection.
🧸
No toys, books, or electronics
If it's not a piece of clothing, we can't take it. Kids' clothes are fine (donated) — everything else isn't.
Got a mix? That's fine — just bag your clothing separately and leave the rest. You don't need to sort clothing into grades; we do that. Just keep non-clothing items out of the bags.
On the day

How the assessment works.

01
We look through everything
We go through your bags at the door, pulling out anything that looks premium. This takes 5–15 minutes depending on the volume.
02
We make you an offer
Premium pieces get individual prices. The rest gets weighed. We give you a total and you decide — no pressure, no obligation to accept.
03
Paid before we leave
If you're happy, we pay via bank transfer on the spot. Everything we take goes with us — nothing gets left behind to sort later.
